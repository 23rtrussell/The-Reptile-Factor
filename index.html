Some starter info about Reptilians.

Crocodiles- There eyes are meant for hunting in the dark as they are a nocturnal hunter. Their eyes have vertical-split pupils as they exclude light more effectively then circular pupils.

Geckos- They can be found in every place except antartica!

Bearded Dragon- While breaded dragons dont look like they would be a good pet they are actually excellent pets for little kids. This is due to their docile nature.

Snake- While snakes may terrify some people they are also excellent pets. As long as they have somewhere to absorb light they won't take much maintenance.
